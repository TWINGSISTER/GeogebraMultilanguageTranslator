This site offer several services to foster the reusability of Geogebra activities. The main offered services are:

  • a translator service that creates augmented ggb activities that adds multi-lingual support to existing mono-lingual GGB activities;
  • a reporter service that adds reporting to existing GGB activities;
  • a collection of augmented ggb activities with multilingual and reporting support see examples here (press LNG) and here (do something then press REPORT).
The augmented ggbs are transparent w.r.t. the Moodle plugins that supports GGB activities with auto-grading in Moodle and extended report in Moodle. Thus we can package multilingual ggb activity with report for self assessed grading into Moodle backups to provide cartridge to load quickly into Moodle collections of  ready to use GGB activities.
